Machina

Flagship Agentic Platform · Type III Core

Machina is the operating system of a Type III healthcare organization. A fully integrated agentic platform delivering autonomous capability across every dimension of the care continuum — from first patient contact to clinical documentation to population health management.

Out of the box, Machina powers omnichannel communications, CRM, intelligent scheduling, agentic calling and messaging, autonomous agent workers, agentic manager-layer orchestration, evaluation frameworks, closed-loop workflow automation, and deep EMR integrations.
