The AI Transition Scale

Where does your organization stand?

Like the Kardashev Scale for civilizations, healthcare organizations are now diverging into distinct tiers based on AI adoption. The gap between tiers will compound over the next 24 months.

I

The Unready

No to low AI adoption

Relying on legacy workflows, manual processes, and disconnected systems. Every task requires human intervention. Costs compound. Talent burns out. Competitive position erodes.

Operating this way in 2026 means running twice as hard just to stay still — while others are sprinting.

⚠ Declining viability
II

The Augmented

High human + AI adoption

Teams have embraced AI tools. Clinicians use AI for documentation. Operators use AI for analysis. Productivity improves. But all workflows still require a human to initiate and close every loop.

Better — but still constrained by human bandwidth. Scale is still linear.

◎ Transitional posture
III

The Autonomous

Human AI + Agentic Workforce

Your human team is fully AI-augmented and an agentic workforce operates alongside them — handling outreach, documentation, scheduling, follow-up, and complex workflows autonomously.

Capacity scales beyond headcount. Response times drop to seconds. Cost per outcome falls dramatically. This is the only viable posture.

The Platform

The infrastructure of a Type III organization.

A complete agentic operating system for healthcare — built once, deployed fast, customized deeply. Every component is integrated. Every workflow is configurable. Every data interaction is compliant.

Built on Elixir/Phoenix and React/TypeScript for performance at any scale.

Agent Factory

Build autonomous AI employees for specific tasks — patient outreach via SMS, telephony triage, documentation, follow-up. Full observability, auditing, and prompt management built in.

Intelligent Workflows & Queues

Personal workspaces route tasks to the right person. Intelligent queues let teams burn down shared work. Priority weighting, SLA tracking, and auto-grab mechanics prevent nothing from falling through the cracks.

Integrated CRM

A fully customizable relationship management system that's native to your platform — not bolted on. Patient relationships, payer relationships, and partner interactions unified in one place.

Omnichannel Communications

SMS, telephony, and video conferencing built in and fully integrated. Click-to-dial, IVR, call routing, in-platform video with external parties — tied directly to scheduling and workflows.

Scheduling & Logistics

Full scheduling for face-to-face visits requiring logistical coordination and virtual appointments. Route optimization, availability constraints, and resource management — all in one system.

Security & Compliance

HIPAA, SOC2 Type II, and HITRUST controls baked into the architecture — not patched on afterward. Integrations with EMRs, ADT vendors, authentication providers, and fax systems are baseline.
